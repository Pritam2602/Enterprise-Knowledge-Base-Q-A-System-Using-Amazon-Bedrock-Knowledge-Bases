1. PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This policy outlines the procedures for submitting, approving, and reimbursing
business-related expenses incurred by employees of TechCorp Industries. All
employees are expected to exercise good judgment and fiscal responsibility when
incurring business expenses.

2. GENERAL PRINCIPLES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- All expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and directly related to business
- Employees should seek the most cost-effective options when making purchases
- Expenses must be supported by original receipts or documentation
- Personal expenses must never be charged to the company
- Fraudulent expense claims will result in immediate disciplinary action

3. APPROVAL AUTHORITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Expense approval limits (pre-approval required above these amounts):
- Individual Contributors: Up to $500 (manager approval)
- Team Leads: Up to $2,000 (director approval)
- Managers: Up to $5,000 (VP approval)
- Directors: Up to $10,000 (SVP approval)
- VPs and above: Up to $25,000 (CFO approval)

Expenses exceeding $25,000 require CEO approval.

4. TRAVEL EXPENSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 Air Travel
- Domestic flights: Economy class for all employees
- International flights under 6 hours: Economy class
- International flights over 6 hours: Business class for Directors and above;
  Premium Economy for all other employees
- Flights must be booked at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Use the corporate travel portal (Concur) for all bookings
- Preferred airlines: United, Delta, American (for corporate rate benefits)

4.2 Hotel Accommodations
Maximum nightly rates (including taxes):
- Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo): $300/night
- Tier 2 cities (Chicago, Seattle, Berlin, Singapore): $250/night
- Tier 3 cities (all others): $200/night
- Extended stays (7+ nights): Contact Travel Desk for negotiated rates
- Hotel bookings must be made through Concur or approved travel agents

4.3 Ground Transportation
- Rental Cars: Compact or midsize vehicles only; SUVs require manager approval
- Ride-sharing (Uber/Lyft): Allowed for business purposes; use Business profiles
- Mileage Reimbursement: $0.67 per mile for personal vehicle use on business
- Parking: Actual costs reimbursed with receipt
- Public transportation: Reimbursed at actual cost

4.4 Meals During Travel
Daily meal allowances (per person):
- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $30
- Dinner: $50
- Total daily maximum: $100

Alcohol is not reimbursable except during approved client entertainment events.

5. CLIENT ENTERTAINMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.1 Guidelines
- Must have a clear business purpose
- Prior manager approval required for expenses over $200
- Documentation must include: attendees, business purpose, and relationship to
  TechCorp
- Maximum per-person spend: $150 for dinner, $75 for lunch, $50 for coffee/drinks

5.2 Gift Policy
- Client gifts must not exceed $100 per recipient per year
- No cash or cash-equivalent gifts (gift cards)
- Government clients: No gifts permitted (compliance with anti-bribery laws)
- All gifts must be reported to the Finance team

6. OFFICE AND SUPPLIES EXPENSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Office supplies: Order through the approved vendor portal (Staples Business)
- Software licenses: Must be approved by IT department
- Books and subscriptions: Up to $200/year without approval; above requires
  manager approval
- Home office equipment (for remote workers): One-time $500 allowance for desk,
  chair, and accessories; $100 annual allowance for supplies

7. SUBMISSION AND REIMBURSEMENT PROCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.1 Submission Requirements
- Submit all expense reports within 30 days of incurring the expense
- Expenses older than 60 days will NOT be reimbursed
- Required documentation for each expense:
  * Original receipt (photo or scanned)
  * Business purpose description
  * Names of attendees (for meals and entertainment)
  * Project or cost center code

7.2 How to Submit
1. Log in to Concur Expense (expense.techcorp.com)
2. Create a new expense report
3. Add individual expense items with receipts
4. Assign appropriate cost center and project codes
5. Submit for manager approval

7.3 Reimbursement Timeline
- Approved expenses are reimbursed within 10 business days
- Reimbursements are deposited directly to the employee's bank account
- International expenses are converted at the exchange rate on the date incurred

8. NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The following expenses will NOT be reimbursed:
- Personal entertainment or leisure activities
- Traffic violations, parking tickets, or fines
- Airline upgrades (unless pre-approved)
- Mini-bar charges
- Laundry (unless travel exceeds 5 consecutive days)
- Spouse or companion travel expenses
- Personal phone charges (unless on an approved BYOD plan)
- First-class air travel (unless medically required with documentation)

For questions, contact the Finance team at expenses@techcorp.com or ext. 2100.
