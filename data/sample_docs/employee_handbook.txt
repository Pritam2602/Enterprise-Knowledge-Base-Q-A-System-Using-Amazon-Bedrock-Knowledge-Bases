1. WELCOME TO TECHCORP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Welcome to TechCorp Industries! We are thrilled to have you join our team. This
handbook provides essential information about our company culture, policies, and
the resources available to help you succeed in your career with us.

Our Mission: To deliver innovative technology solutions that empower businesses
and improve lives.

Our Values:
- Innovation: We embrace creativity and push the boundaries of technology
- Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency in everything we do
- Collaboration: We achieve more together through teamwork
- Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in our work
- Inclusion: We celebrate diversity and foster a welcoming environment

2. ONBOARDING PROCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 First Day Checklist
Your onboarding coordinator will guide you through the following:
- [ ] Receive employee ID badge and building access card
- [ ] Set up workstation (laptop, monitors, peripherals)
- [ ] Complete IT account setup (email, Slack, Jira, GitHub)
- [ ] Review and sign required documents (NDA, IP agreement, handbook acknowledgment)
- [ ] Meet your team and direct supervisor
- [ ] Tour of the office and facilities

2.2 First Week
- Complete mandatory compliance training (2 hours)
- Complete security awareness training (1 hour)
- Meet with your manager to review 30/60/90 day goals
- Join relevant Slack channels and distribution groups
- Attend New Hire Orientation session (every Friday, 2-4 PM)

2.3 Probationary Period
All new employees undergo a 90-day probationary period during which:
- Performance is evaluated at 30, 60, and 90 days
- Employment may be terminated with one week's notice by either party
- Benefits eligibility begins on Day 1, but some benefits (401k match) begin
  after the probationary period

3. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Performance Reviews
- Annual Review: Conducted in March, includes self-assessment, manager evaluation,
  and peer feedback (360-degree review)
- Mid-Year Check-In: Conducted in September, informal progress review
- Quarterly Goals: Set collaboratively with your manager each quarter

3.2 Rating Scale
5 - Exceptional: Consistently exceeds all expectations
4 - Exceeds Expectations: Regularly exceeds job requirements
3 - Meets Expectations: Fulfills all job requirements satisfactorily
2 - Needs Improvement: Falls below expectations in some areas
1 - Unsatisfactory: Does not meet minimum job requirements

3.3 Career Development
TechCorp Industries is committed to employee growth. Resources include:
- Individual Development Plans (IDPs) created with your manager
- Internal Job Postings: Employees with 12+ months tenure can apply for transfers
- Mentorship Program: Pairing with senior employees for career guidance
- Conference Budget: Up to $2,500 per year for approved industry conferences
- Training Budget: $5,000 per year for courses, certifications, and workshops

4. WORKPLACE CULTURE AND FACILITIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 Office Facilities
- Cafeteria: Subsidized meals available 8 AM - 2 PM (employees pay 30% of cost)
- Fitness Center: Free gym on Floors B1, open 6 AM - 10 PM
- Game Room: Pool table, foosball, board games (available during breaks)
- Quiet Rooms: Reservable rooms for focused work or personal calls
- Mother's Room: Private rooms on each floor for nursing mothers
- Parking: Free parking in designated employee lots (register at Front Desk)

4.2 Communication Tools
- Email: Microsoft Outlook (primary formal communication)
- Instant Messaging: Slack (day-to-day team communication)
- Video Conferencing: Microsoft Teams (meetings and presentations)
- Project Management: Jira and Confluence
- Code Collaboration: GitHub Enterprise

4.3 Company Events
- Town Hall Meetings: Monthly all-hands meeting with leadership (first Monday)
- Team Building: Quarterly team activities (budget: $50 per employee)
- Annual Company Retreat: 3-day offsite event in October
- Holiday Party: Annual celebration in December
- Hack Days: Bi-annual innovation days where employees work on passion projects

5. HEALTH AND WELLNESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.1 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
Free, confidential counseling and support services available 24/7:
- Mental health counseling (up to 8 sessions per year)
- Financial planning advice
- Legal consultation
- Work-life balance coaching
Phone: 1-800-555-HELP | Online: eap.techcorp.com

5.2 Wellness Programs
- Annual Health Screening: Free on-site health checks in June
- Flu Vaccinations: Free for all employees and their families
- Mental Health Days: 3 additional paid days per year for mental wellness
- Ergonomic Assessment: Free workstation evaluation by certified ergonomist
- Wellness Challenges: Monthly step challenges, meditation programs

6. EXIT PROCEDURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6.1 Resignation
Employees wishing to resign must provide:
- Individual Contributors: 2 weeks written notice
- Managers: 4 weeks written notice
- Directors and above: 8 weeks written notice

6.2 Exit Process
- Submit resignation letter to your manager and HR
- Complete knowledge transfer documentation
- Return all company property (laptop, badge, keys, equipment)
- Complete exit interview with HR
- Final paycheck and unused PTO payout within 14 days

For any questions, please contact your HR Business Partner or email hr@techcorp.com.
