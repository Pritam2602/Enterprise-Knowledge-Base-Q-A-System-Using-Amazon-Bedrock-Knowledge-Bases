1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TechCorp Industries recognizes the importance of work-life balance and provides
a comprehensive leave program for all eligible employees. This policy outlines
the various types of leave available, eligibility requirements, and the
procedures for requesting time off.

2. ANNUAL LEAVE (PAID TIME OFF - PTO)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 Entitlement
PTO accrual is based on length of service:
- 0-2 years of service:   18 days per year (1.5 days/month)
- 3-5 years of service:   22 days per year (1.83 days/month)
- 6-10 years of service:  26 days per year (2.17 days/month)
- 10+ years of service:   30 days per year (2.5 days/month)

2.2 Accrual and Usage Rules
- PTO begins accruing from the first day of employment
- PTO can be used after completing 30 days of employment
- Minimum usage: Half-day (4 hours) increments
- Maximum consecutive days: 15 business days (additional requires VP approval)
- PTO cannot be taken during company-designated blackout periods (year-end close,
  major product launches)

2.3 Carry-Over Policy
- Up to 5 unused PTO days may be carried over to the next calendar year
- Carried-over days must be used by March 31 of the following year
- Days exceeding the carry-over limit will be forfeited on December 31
- Upon resignation or termination, accrued but unused PTO will be paid out
  at the employee's regular rate

3. SICK LEAVE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Entitlement
- All full-time employees: 12 sick days per year
- Part-time employees: Prorated based on scheduled hours
- Sick leave does not carry over to the next year
- Unused sick leave is not paid out upon separation

3.2 Usage Guidelines
- Sick leave may be used for personal illness, injury, or medical appointments
- Sick leave may also be used to care for an immediate family member who is ill
  (spouse, child, parent)
- For absences of 3 or more consecutive days, a doctor's note is required
- Employees must notify their manager before their scheduled start time
- Chronic absenteeism may be reviewed under the attendance policy

4. PARENTAL LEAVE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 Maternity Leave
- 16 weeks of paid leave at 100% of base salary
- Additional 8 weeks of unpaid leave available upon request
- Leave begins on the date of birth or the start of pre-delivery complications
- Must provide 30 days advance notice when possible

4.2 Paternity Leave
- 8 weeks of paid leave at 100% of base salary
- Additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave available upon request
- Must be taken within 12 months of birth or adoption
- Must provide 30 days advance notice when possible

4.3 Adoption Leave
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks of paid leave at 100% of base salary
- Secondary caregiver: 8 weeks of paid leave at 100% of base salary
- Leave begins on the date of placement or official adoption proceedings required

4.4 Return to Work
- Employees returning from parental leave are guaranteed their same position
  or an equivalent role
- Phased return options available: 3 weeks of 50% schedule at full pay
- Lactation rooms available on every floor of the office

5. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Paid bereavement leave is provided as follows:
- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 business days
- Extended family (grandparent, grandchild, in-law): 3 business days
- Close friend or colleague: 1 business day

Additional unpaid leave may be granted at manager discretion for travel or
extended grieving needs.

6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TechCorp Industries observes the following paid public holidays (10 days):
1. New Year's Day (January 1)
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day (Third Monday in January)
3. Presidents' Day (Third Monday in February)
4. Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
5. Independence Day (July 4)
6. Labor Day (First Monday in September)
7. Thanksgiving Day (Fourth Thursday in November)
8. Day after Thanksgiving (Fourth Friday in November)
9. Christmas Eve (December 24)
10. Christmas Day (December 25)

When a holiday falls on a weekend, the nearest weekday is observed.
Employees required to work on holidays receive 1.5x pay or a floating holiday.

7. OTHER LEAVE TYPES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.1 Jury Duty
- Fully paid leave for the duration of jury service
- Employee must provide court documentation
- Must return to work when court is not in session

7.2 Voting Leave
- Up to 2 hours of paid leave on Election Day
- Must notify manager at least 3 days in advance

7.3 Military Leave
- Up to 15 days of paid leave per year for military reserve duties
- Extended military leave follows applicable federal and state laws (USERRA)
- Position is guaranteed upon return from military service

7.4 Sabbatical Leave
- Available to employees with 7+ years of continuous service
- 4 weeks of paid sabbatical leave
- Must be approved by VP and HR at least 3 months in advance
- Available once every 7 years

7.5 Unpaid Leave of Absence
- Available for personal reasons not covered by other leave types
- Maximum duration: 30 days per year
- Requires manager and HR approval
- Benefits continue during unpaid leave up to 30 days
- Position guarantee applies for approved leaves

8. LEAVE REQUEST PROCEDURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Submit leave request through the HR Portal (hr.techcorp.com)
Step 2: Manager reviews and approves/declines within 3 business days
Step 3: Approved leave is reflected in the company calendar
Step 4: HR records are updated automatically

Advance notice requirements:
- PTO: At least 5 business days for 1-2 day requests
         At least 15 business days for 3+ day requests
- Sick Leave: Before shift start (same day)
- Parental Leave: 30 days when possible
- Sabbatical: 3 months

For questions about leave policies, contact HR at leave@techcorp.com or ext. 1500.
